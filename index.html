Privacy Policy for Hyper Bounce

Effective Date: June 29, 2026

Last Updated: June 29, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Hyper Bounce collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile game application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • AdMob Advertising: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device information, advertising ID, and usage data to show relevant ads.
  • Game Data: Gameplay statistics, progress, settings, and unlocked content stored locally on your device.
  • Technical Information: Device type, operating system version, and app performance data.

2.2 Optional Features

  • Google Play Games Services: If you choose to sign in, your Play Games display name and scores may be shared with Google to show leaderboards. Sign-in is optional and the game works without it.
  • In-App Purchases: The "Remove Ads" purchase is processed by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment information.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Precise location data
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Contact list or personal files

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve the game experience
  • Display advertisements through AdMob
  • Analyze app performance and fix technical issues
  • Save your game progress and settings locally
  • Show optional leaderboards via Google Play Games

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share limited technical data with Google AdMob and Google Play Games Services, subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is family-friendly but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

6. Data Security

Game progress is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction of data.

7. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob and Google Play Games Services. See the Google Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy

8. Your Choices

  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's advertising settings.
  • Remove Ads: A one-time purchase removes banner and interstitial ads.
  • App Permissions: You can revoke app permissions through your device settings.
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored game data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the Last Updated date at the top of this policy.
